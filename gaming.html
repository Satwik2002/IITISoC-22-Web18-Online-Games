ABOUT
SCOT is a free browser gaming website. SCOT exclusively contains multiplayer games like, scribbl, connect four, Othello, and tic-tac-toe. You can enjoy playing fun games without interruptions from downloads, intrusive ads, or pop-ups. Just load up your favorite games instantly in your web browser and enjoy the experience. Not just this, you can also invite your friends into a room or join another room. You can play our games on desktop or mobile devices. That includes everything from desktop PCs, laptops, and Chromebooks, to the latest smartphones and tablets from Apple and Android.
